### Benefits of New Grid
1. **Auto-fit**: Automatically adjusts columns based on available space
2. **Minmax**: Ensures cards are at least 160px wide
3. **Max-width**: Prevents cards from being too wide
4. **Centered**: Uses margin: 0 auto for centering
5. **Justify-items**: Centers items within grid cells
